A [[recipe]].

* 1pt onion
* 2pt mushrooms
* crème fraîche
* tarragon

Serve with rice or orzo.

Fry onion until golden. Add mushrooms, cook until starting to release their moisture. Turn down heat, add crème fraîche and tarragon. Serve immediately.
